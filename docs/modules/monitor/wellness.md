# Employee Wellness Monitoring

Monitor tracks work patterns to proactively identify burnout risk, overwork, and work-life balance issues. Wellness metrics help managers support their teams before burnout impacts productivity and retention.

## Wellness Dashboard

Team-level overview of work-life balance indicators:

- **Average daily hours** -- Mean active hours per day across the team
- **Overtime percentage** -- Percentage of team members exceeding configured daily/weekly thresholds
- **Weekend workers** -- Number of team members active on weekends
- **Break compliance** -- Percentage of team members taking regular breaks
- **After-hours trend** -- 30-day trend of after-hours work across the team

## Individual Wellness

Per-user wellness metrics and recommendations:

- **Daily work pattern** -- Start time, end time, active hours, break frequency
- **Weekly hours** -- Total active hours vs. configured expected hours
- **Overtime history** -- Days and weeks where thresholds were exceeded
- **Break patterns** -- Frequency and duration of breaks throughout the workday
- **Work-life balance score** -- Composite score based on hours, overtime, breaks, and after-hours activity

## Wellness Alerts

Automated alerts based on configurable thresholds:

- **Daily overwork** -- User active for more than X hours in a single day (default: 10)
- **Weekly overwork** -- User active for more than X hours in a week (default: 50)
- **No break** -- User has been continuously active for more than X hours without a break (default: 4)
- **Weekend work** -- User active on Saturday or Sunday
- **Increasing after-hours** -- Trend of increasing late-night or early-morning activity over the past 2 weeks

Alerts are sent to the user's direct manager and optionally to HR.
